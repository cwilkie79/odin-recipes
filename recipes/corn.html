Corn on the Cob

Description

This recipe is so easy to make, and always such a hit! It is great for backyard barbecues. For best results, chill overnight.

Ingredients

  • Corn on cob

Steps

  1. Pull back the husks, remove the silk, then coat the corn with butter, salt, and pepper. Close the husks and wrap each ear of corn in foil.
  2. Grill each piece of corn on the cob until tender (turn the corn occasionally to ensure even cooking).
